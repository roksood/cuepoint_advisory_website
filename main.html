Who We Are

Judgment Before Execution

CuePoint is an advisory partner to healthcare and life sciences leaders navigating structural change. We work with executives accountable for access, growth, and long-term positioning — when the path forward is unclear and the stakes are high.

Most firms produce deliverables. We produce decisions. Our value is strategic translation — moving fluidly between payer economics, employer benefit design, patient behavior, and policy dynamics to frame choices others present as isolated problems.

"The most dangerous strategy decisions aren't the ones that look risky — they're the ones that look safe because no one questioned the assumptions."
Principle One

Simplistic

Strip away inherited complexity to find what is essential. Healthcare strategies accumulate layers — legacy assumptions, outdated contracts, organizational inertia. We start by asking which constraints are real and which are inherited.

Principle Two

Humanistic

Design around how stakeholders actually behave, not how the system assumes they should. When a strategy optimizes for organizational logic instead of the reality of patients, payers, and providers — it solves for the model, not the market.

Complexity is not rigor. And efficiency without empathy is just cost-cutting.

Market access decisions are no longer linear, and the cost of waiting for clarity is rising. The constraint is no longer data — it is strategic translation across a system that no longer moves one piece at a time.
